Racial stereotypes

  • The effects of racial stereotypes in education include biased assumptions about students' abilities, behavior, and potential based on their race or ethnicity.
  • These stereotypes often lead to lower expectations for students from marginalized racial groups, impacting academic performance and limiting opportunities for growth. Students subjected to racial biases may feel alienated, discouraged, or undervalued, which can harm their confidence and motivation.
