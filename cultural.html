Cultural Stereotypes

  • Cultural stereotypes in education involve biased assumptions about a student’s abilities, behavior, or potential based on their cultural background, traditions, or ethnic identity. click here to get a video on Cultural Stereotypes
  • These stereotypes often result in misjudgments, limiting students' opportunities and academic growth. Educators may unknowingly lower expectations for students from certain cultural groups, while students from dominant cultures might receive preferential treatment. Such biases can hinder inclusion, reinforce inequality, and negatively affects classroom dynamics.
