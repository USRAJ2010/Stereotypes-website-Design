Gender Stereotypes

  • The effects of gender stereotypes in education include biased assumptions about students' abilities, behavior, and potential based on their gender. click here to get a video on Gender Stereotypes
  • These stereotypes often lead to unequal treatment, where boys may be encouraged in subjects like math and science, while girls might be steered toward humanities. Such biases can restrict students' academic choices and limit their future opportunities. Gender stereotypes also reinforce traditional roles, affecting students' confidence and performance. Over time, these biases contribute to the gender gap in various fields, perpetuating inequality and limiting the potential for a balanced, inclusive educational environment.
